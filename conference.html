< Saturday Oct 9th 2021 >

Go to agenda

Odias in ML Conference 2021 is the second international congregation of Odias techies in AI/ML, academicians, policymakers, linguists, business executives, investors, entrepreneurs and those working towards positively intervening in people’s lives through AI/ML.

The conference, divided into three sessions on the topics ecosystem, entrepreneurship, and skills to scale, will try to set the foundation for greater engagement in the future for specific action points that will emerge from the deliberations.
